About us

ABOUT US

Children book publishers are encountering the major ‘BRUNT’ of Information Age. Every year they have to come out with ‘some thing new and different’ to counter the competition. Therefore, to catapult your growth in this competition and add dynamism to your publications Kohli Ads come out with three pronged CREATIVE, INNOVATIVE & EDUCATIVE approach to serve you professionally and efficiently. Keeping this in view our versatile founder Sh. Pritpal Singh has gathered like minded people under one roof to offer holistic solution to every aspect Children Books Publisher. We are equipped with state of the art technologies & tools of publishing industry to meet this challenge.

What we do ?

Introductory books for toddlers. Books with colourful attractive titles & inside short stories with expressive large sized illustrations designed for kids to understand their environment through pictures, rhymes, activities, etc. Curriculum based books meant for the students of LKG, UKG, Nursery and 1st to 8th standard. Complying with the syllabus either of NCERT or CCE. General books are abyss of easy to understand & interactive information. The deeper you dive more precious technical data, knowledge & information you get. Eye catching, informative, interactive & expressive TITLES. Attracting parents & children alike to pick up the book and buy it. Our each department and every single step in the entire creative process bears in mind the repute, quality and scheduling requirements that we need to deliver.
